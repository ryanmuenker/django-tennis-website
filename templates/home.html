About Tennis

Tennis is a globally popular sport that involves intense physical activity, strategic thinking, and precision. Played between two players (singles) or two pairs (doubles), the game involves hitting a ball over a net into the opponent’s court to score points.

From its origins in 12th-century France to modern-day grand slams like Wimbledon and the US Open, tennis has evolved into a symbol of elegance and athleticism. It’s a sport that inspires millions worldwide, combining individual skill with competitive spirit.
